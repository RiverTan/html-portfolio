Intro

This project was created in the WNM 695 Automotive UI class in Spring 2018. The client, FCA offered us two brands to choose from, the Maserati and the Alfa Romeo. Our team chose to build a sports and luxury car for Maserati. I am in charge of the rendering and animation in this team project.
