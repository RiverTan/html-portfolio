Intro

Design Talk is a design conference held by Shopee in Singapore, aiming to share the design inspections and thoughts from Shopee design team to the whole company and public. It is held every year in September.

Logo Exploration

The three keywords for 2017 conference was share, teamwork, and trustworthy. Based on the keywords, I developed nine logos from different visual directions.

Final Logo

The logo was inspired by the graphs of the soundtrack. It was a combination of repeating forms, but with different heights. The graphs gave people an instinctive reaction of sound.

Logo Transformation

I disassembled the logo and made each form lay down. The result looked like a morse code. After playing around the new form, I rearranged and shaped them to be circle, triangle, and rectangle, representing the feelings of the three keywords.

Posters

They had three posters as a set, each representing a keyword for the design conference.
