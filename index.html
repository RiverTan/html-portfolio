ABOUT ME

A creative visual storyteller with more than 5 years in multi-disciplines of design.

Hello, I‘m River Tan. A creative and hard-working designer with a passion for creating user-centered design solutions. I mainly focus on visual design, visual strategy, and user interaction.
As a visual storyteller, I aim for user's instinctive reaction to the beauty of the object itself. However, I believe that good aesthetics is not enough for a great design. Instead, it is the balance of usability, feasibility, and simplicity that enables better user experience.
